The Problem

Enterprises weren't failing at automation. They were failing at coordination.

By the time Boomi's customers started deploying AI agents alongside their existing integrations, a painful pattern had emerged — the Automation Silo Trap. A company might run Boomi Integration to sync ERP data, Data Hub to maintain customer records, and Agentstudio to power an AI service bot. Each piece worked. None of them talked to each other.

The result was "Agent Amnesia" — when an Inventory Agent handed a task to a Billing Agent, context disappeared. IT teams were left manually stitching five separate systems together using humans as the bridge.

"Before Orchestrate, customers weren't just losing data — they were losing the intent of their business workflows. Integrations could move data from point A to B, but they couldn't coordinate a smart, multi-step business conclusion across humans and AI."

A Concrete Example: The Logistics Nightmare

Before Orchestrate

Five separate loops ran simultaneously, completely blind to each other — IoT tracking, API management, vendor data, a customer chatbot, and an operations risk agent.

When a storm delayed a major shipment, Loop 5 flagged the risk but couldn't reach Loop 4. The customer chatbot kept saying everything was on time. Human operators had to manually pull data from the ERP, email the customs broker, and alert the client themselves.

With Orchestrate

Orchestrate sits above all five loops as the conductor. When IoT data flags a weather delay, it instantly pulls clean vendor data, passes context to the operations AI, calculates a new delivery window, alerts the customer chatbot proactively, and routes a financial approval to a human manager if penalties apply — all automatically.

My Role

What I owned

Orchestrate was one of the most cross-functional products I've worked on — 4 PMs, 3 EMs, 4 POs, and 35+ engineers, with senior leadership deeply involved throughout. Within that structure, I owned three specific areas:

Navigation & Workspace UI

The canvas experience where users design, visualise, and manage orchestration workflows. Making a genuinely complex system feel approachable without hiding its power.

Asset Connections

How users discover and connect Boomi's existing capabilities — integrations, APIs, data models, AI agents — onto the Orchestrate canvas. The bridge between what customers already had and what Orchestrate made possible.

AI Integration

Connecting Boomi Agentstudio to the Orchestrate canvas — giving AI agents the power to execute multi-system processes while keeping humans in the loop.
